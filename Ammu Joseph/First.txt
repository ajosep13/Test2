<P>
Kerala is famous for ecotourism. It also contains beautiful mountains, valleys and gorges.
Kerala lies between Arabian Sea in the east and western Ghats in the west.
</P>

<P>
The Kerala staple food revolves around an abundance of coconut, rice, tapioca, and spices like black pepper, cloves, cinnamon, and ginger. 
Kerala famous food also features a variety of seafood like fish, mussels, crabs, lobsters,crabs, and prawns owing to the state's long coastline.
